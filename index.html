About Us

The Solar Society of Pohang is a forum for inquisitive minds from all walks of life â€“ scientists, engineers, artists, entrepreneurs, ethicists, philosophers, and more. We believe that by bringing together diverse perspectives, we can spark new ideas, foster collaboration, and address the complex challenges facing our world today.

Inspired by historical societies like the Lunar Society of Birmingham, we aim to cultivate an environment of intellectual curiosity and open dialogue. We are particularly interested in exploring the transformative potential of artificial intelligence and its impact on various aspects of human life, from industry and economy to art and ethics.

Our meetings feature engaging presentations, lively discussions, and interactive workshops, providing a platform for members to share their knowledge, learn from others, and contribute to a brighter future.

Our Mission

The Solar Society of Pohang brings together diverse experts to foster innovation and address contemporary challenges, with a focus on how emerging technologies can benefit society.

Who We Are

We are a group of 10-15 core members from various fields including AI, physics, music, engineering, economics, finance, arts, philosophies, literatures, history, ethics, and social sciences. Our members come from academia & industry across Korea and internationally.

Membership

We carefully curate our membership to ensure a diverse range of expertise and perspectives. Potential members can apply or be nominated.

What We Do

  • Hold bi-annual meetings
  • Discuss cutting-edge topics and their societal implications
  • Propose collaborative projects between academia and industry
  • Organize public lectures to share insights with the community
