Our Mission

The Solar Society of Pohang brings together diverse experts to foster innovation and address contemporary challenges, with a focus on how emerging technologies can benefit society.

Sun Rising Alliance

The South Korean coastal cities of Ulsan, Pohang, and Gyeongju, located along the eastern seaboard, have established the "Sun Rising Alliance." This collaborative initiative aptly reflects the name of our society, given these cities' strategic position to greet the dawn.

Who We Are

We are a group of 10-15 core members from various fields including AI, physics, music, engineering, economics, finance, legal, arts, philosophies, literatures, history, ethics, and social sciences. Our members come from academia & industry across Korea and internationally.

Membership

We carefully curate our membership to ensure a diverse range of expertise and perspectives. Potential members can apply or be nominated.

What We Do

  • Hold bi-annual meetings
  • Discuss cutting-edge topics and their societal implications
  • Propose collaborative projects between academia and industry
  • Organize public lectures to share insights with the community

Join Us

If you are passionate about interdisciplinary collaboration and the role of technology in society, we'd love to hear from you. Contact us at solar.society.pohang@gmail.com to learn more about attending a meeting or becoming a member.
